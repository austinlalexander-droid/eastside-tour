Eastside Gathering Information

 

The leadership of Eastside Community Church has been prayerfully considering the safest and most appropriate ways to gather based on the guidance of local and state governing authorities and medical professionals. We are eager to gather together again as a body of believers, but hope to do so in wisdom. Below you will find our plan for gathering together as a church. 

Worship Services:

  • Wearing a face covering is optional at both of our services. Feel free to do whatever makes you feel most comfortable.

  • Weekly registration is no longer required to attend services or for your children to attend Eastside Kids ministry.

  • Instead of the auditorium at Conrad, Eastside will temporarily host multiple smaller services at Scofield Church on Sunday afternoons at 1:30 & 3:30 pm. We love Conrad HS and look forward to being back there, but DISD is not currently renting out their facilities
